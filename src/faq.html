Can I browse in store?
Unfortunately, we don't have a display at the moment; our location is only for picking up orders. This may change in the near future, however...
Is in store pickup available?
Yes, please select that option when viewing your cart.
When are you open?
We're open from 18:00 to 22:00, Monday through Saturday.

SHIPPING

How do you ship?
We primarily use Canada Post to ship our books. You can choose between the Expedited Parcel delivery standard or the XpressPost delivery standard.
How much does shipping cost?
Shipping costs are calculated at purchase time, based on Canada Post rates. Shipping is free for purchases over $150!
Do you ship outside Manitoba/Internationally?
Yes we do! We cannot predict any customs or duty fees, and those would be the sole responisbility of the customer.
Are shipments tracked/insured?
Canada Post parcels are tracked, and Xpresspost parcels are insured up to $100 in value.

PAYMENT

How can I pay?
We accept all major credit cards or e-payment methods. Crypto currency support coming soon!
Are payments secure?
Of course: we use the latest in Payment Security Systems and comply with all Industry standards.
Do you accept giftcards?
We don't issue any giftcards, and we can't accept other gift cards either.
Are prices in USD?
No, all prices are listed in Canadian Dollars. International customers will be charged in their own currencies, however the exchange rate and fees are determined by their payment processors.
Do you take trade ins?
At the moment we do not accept trade ins. If you believe you have something exceptional, feel free to contact us.

RETURNS

Do you accept returns?
Returns are accepted for new items. Used books are final sale.
How long is the return window?
Returns on new items are available for thirty days after receiving the item.
Do I have to pay for return shipping?
No. However, a 15% restocking fee is charged.

REQUEST-A-TITLE

What is Request-a-Title?
The Request-a-Title feature is a way for you to get the hard to find titles as fast as possible. We leverage our experience and publisher connections in order to serve you faster. CLICK HERE to learn more.
How long does it take for a request to be filled?
This is very variable and dependent on the request. Most queries are fulfilled within three weeks.
Can I cancel my request?
Yes, please email us or use the CONTACT form.
