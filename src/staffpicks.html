John's Pick

My pick of the month is Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs by Abelson ans Sussman. This classic text will expand your programming knowledge to the next level, reveal the underlying secrets of interpreter, compiler, and Programing Language design, all while using the most powerful programming language, Lisp. Check out this classic lecture!

Henry's Pick

For this month, I suggest Agile: An Essential Guide to Agile Project Management by James Edge. From my personal experience, the principles laid out in this book can organize a disorganized team, which leads to faster development spoeeds and a drive to fulfil customer satisfaction.
